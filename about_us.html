About Rust Reloaded

Rust Reloaded is a thrilling 10x PVE/PVP Rust server designed for intense gameplay and community fun. We feature purge days for epic battles, zombies to add extra challenge, and compatibility with the most popular Oxide plugins.

Players can grow ganja using our custom ganja plugin, and manage farms effortlessly with the FarmPro mod, which enables Bluetooth farming for automated and convenient resource management.

Founded by passionate Rust enthusiasts, our goal is to provide a balanced, exciting environment where players can build, raid, and survive in a vibrant community.
